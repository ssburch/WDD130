Arizona

Arizona is the 48th state in the United States, located in the western region. Arizona is home to the Grand Canyon, which is one of the 7 Natural Wonders of the World. The Sonoran Desert is also the only place where the saguaro cactus grows. The hottest temperature ever recorded in Arizona was in 1994 in Lake Havasu City where the temperature was 128 degrees!
