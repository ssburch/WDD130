Overview

Purpose

Welcome to Sunset Rafting! We understand the 9-5 grind, and know that adventurers work desk jobs too. At Sunset Rafting, we aim to make the outdoors accessible after office hours. Don't feel like your time is lost after the work day. Meet us at sunset and your experience awaits!

Audience

Who are the target customers?: Our target audience is office employees looking for an adventure after business hours. For those who want to take advantage of their time after the work day, Sunset Rafting is for you! What do they want? Our audience wants accessibility to outdoor activities on their schedule. We all love "fun in the sun", but our customers know that the best rafting comes with a sunset view. How will my audience usually access my site? Our audience is filled with on-the-go types, so most people will view by their own convenient mobile device.
